About Me

Born on the trail

I've been riding since I was 16 — first on forest trails, then on official DH tracks. Today I compete in the national championship and race in the Europe.

My philosophy is simple: technique before speed, speed before ego. Every training session is a line analysis, every race a test of the limits.

Off the track, I work with young riders, helping them take their first steps in the sport.
